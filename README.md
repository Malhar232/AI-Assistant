# AI-Assistant

Hi I am MOJO your personal assistant!

Features:
-Send Emails
-Open browser
-Search a channel on Youtube
-Ask the time
-Get results from wikipedia
-play music on your machine
and more.

Modules required (pip install):
-pyttsx3
-webbrowser
-speechrecognition
-wikipedia

Buit-in modules:
-datetime
-os
-smtplib
